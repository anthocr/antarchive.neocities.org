I remember finally convincing my mom to buy me the game and spent way too much time playing immediately. This page showcases some of my favorite memories and experiencing playing the game.

Minecraft is a game about placing blocks to build anything you can imagine. At night monsters come out, make sure to build a shelter before that happens. It also has music by C418! So far 186,616,953 people have registered and 307,577,279 people bought the game. More stats here.


Videos

Minecraft videos have contibuted significantly to the game's popularity and success, especially on YouTube. The early 2010's were arguably the game's peak on YouTube inspiring people to create. Many different genres of videos soon emerged such as: mod showcases, let's plays, parody songs, and roleplaying. The possiblities were only limited to ways people could play the game, which seemed limitless.

As expected, many popular creators also emerged. Creators such as the Yogscast, SkyDoesMinecraft, BajanCanadian, PopularMMOs, CaptainSparklez, and much much more pionered Minecraft Youtube and became know as MCYTs, though this term was only attributed more recently. Many of these creators have faded into obscurity or controversy.

These are some of the vidoes I consider classics that still hold up to this day. If you were there for this era, you might find some appreciation and/or nostalgia in these videos.
